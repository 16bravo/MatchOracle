About MatchOracle

MatchOracle is a project dedicated to providing up-to-date and historical rankings for national football teams worldwide. The site offers interactive tables, statistics, and visualizations based on official match results and advanced ranking algorithms.

Features:

  • World ranking for all national teams, updated after every international match
  • Historical rankings since 1872
  • Detailed team pages and match results
  • Probability analysis for competitions and fixtures
  • Responsive and user-friendly interface

Data source: All match data and rankings are based on the All International Football Results Dataset available on Kaggle.
